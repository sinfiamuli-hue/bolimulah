Our Heritage

Preserving the Authentic Beats of Maldives

Hailing from the culturally rich island of M. Mulah, Ekuveri Boli Mulah is a premier cultural ensemble dedicated to keeping traditional Maldivian performing arts alive. With deep roots in ancestral rhythms, we deliver raw energy, synchronized choreography, and powerful vocal arrangements that captivate audiences worldwide.
